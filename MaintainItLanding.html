Does Maintain-It work offline? +

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device, so you can access your maintenance records, add tasks, and check off completed items even without an internet connection. Internet is only needed for VIN scanning and accessing NHTSA vehicle databases.

How secure is my data? +

Your data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption. We never upload your maintenance records, vehicle information, or property details to our servers. Your data is yours alone‚Äîwe can't access it, and we never sell it.
